    • A yast module to teach hackers how to cook delicious Chinese food

    Tags: YaST, hackers-food, JudgeMe, JudgeDesktop, JudgeFunny, Done, InBuildService, TryMe

    Description

    Write a Yast module, to schedule what you eat for breakfast, lunch and dinner. All the dish will be selected from more than 800 most famous Chinese food automatically. Once you decide what to eat for your meal, you can choose whether to buy it from restaurant or do it yourself. For the first choice, a eMap will display the position and telephone number of the restaurant where you can buy the food. For the second choice, a video will pop up and teach you how to cook the delicious food by yourself.
