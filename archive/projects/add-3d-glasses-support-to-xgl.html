    • Add 3D Glasses support to XGL

    Tags: Idea, pimpmydesktop, 3d

    Description

    What would be really cool in XGL is that if you could "enable" 3D Glasses support so if you were wearing Anaglyph 3D Glasses then you could have the windows come towards you and away from you when you move the windows around. The pain is that you loose color due to having a red/blue getting cut out by the glasses. But it would still be cool.

    People

    Peter Lambrechtsen originated this idea. No one is currently working on implementing this idea (it's up for grabs!).
