    • Improve openSUSE website with more product-information, e.g. a screenshot-slider

    Tags: Idea, website, wiki, gallery, screenshots

    Description

    The openSUSE frontpage needs more product information, currently it is much too simplistic. A new and interested visitor should get a quick understanding of what openSUSE is and what it can do.

    The openSUSE WIKI frontpage should also feature a screenshot gallery for visual demonstrations.

    The gallery could be implemented as an slider-like application, which loads the featured screenshots from a dynamic backend. Clicking a screenshot enlarges it and shows a brief description.
