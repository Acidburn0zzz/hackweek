    • Add bluetooth headset support

    Tags: Desktop, openSUSE, SLED, Idea, wireless

    Description

    With many other OS you can use bluetooth headsets just 'out of the box'. With openSUSE/SLED you can't. The feature is requested for more than a year now. I think it's time to implement.

    People

    Holger Sickenberg originated this idea. No one is currently working on implementing this idea (it's up for grabs!).
