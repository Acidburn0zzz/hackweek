    • Allow FATE Commenting by E-Mail

    Tags: Idea, DevTool, mail, FATE

    Description

    FATE sends out mails when stuff gets changed. Often I just want to comment on the change, and have to wait until I'm network-connected, start FATE, log in, etc. just to post a comment. The idea here is to enhance FATE so that you can just reply to a FATE mail with some text, and have FATE append it to the entry as a comment.

    Note that this does not propose to add the full functionality of FATE to an e-mail interface, that would be about as much fun to use as Majordomo :-( this is just the special case of wanting to post a comment.

    People

    Crispin Cowan originated this idea. No one is currently working on implementing this idea (it's up for grabs!).
