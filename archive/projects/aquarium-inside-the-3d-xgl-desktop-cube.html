    • Aquarium inside the 3D Xgl desktop cube

    Tags: Idea, Desktop, Graphics, openSUSE, SLED, pimpmydesktop, 3d

    Description

    The 3D desktop of SLED has a very high coolness factor already. But that could be greatly improved upon for on-stage presentation as well as for regular showing off the desktop if the 3D cube of workspaces was filled with a virtual aquarium with swimming fish.

    The aquarium would turn as the desktop is turned when switching workspaces, showing the fish from different sides as they swim.

    There already are 3D aquarium screensavers (eg. atlantis in xscreensaver), so it's just about integrating that together ...

    People

    Vojtech Pavlik originated this idea. Compuz-Fusion developers already got working gears inside the cube so aquarium will be arriving soon too.
