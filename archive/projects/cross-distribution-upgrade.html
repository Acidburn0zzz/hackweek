    • Cross-distribution upgrade

    Tags: Idea, crossplatform

    Description

    YaST is able to upgrade from one SUSE version to a later one. This logic could be extended to upgrade from a non-SUSE distribution to SUSE. So users could upgrade their Fedora 6 to openSUSE 10.3 or even from Ubuntu to openSUSE. Maybe it would also work better to use a completely different approach than the YaST upgrade.

    People

    Cornelius Schumacher originated this idea. No one is currently working on implementing this idea (it's up for grabs!).
