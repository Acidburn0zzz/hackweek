    • Desktop optimization with powertop

    Tags: Idea, Desktop, LowPower

    Description

    PowerTOP is a tool that lets you discover which processes are causing your system to enter higher-power states and drain the battery faster.

    A systematic profiling a SUSE desktop system to find these areas and improve them could extend battery life dramatically with a small number of patches.

    People

    Nat Friedman originated this idea.
