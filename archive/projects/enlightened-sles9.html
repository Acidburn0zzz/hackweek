    • Enlightened SLES9

    Tags: InProgress, Kernel

    Description

    While we have para-virtualized SLES9, there is internal debate on if it is worthwhile supporting yet another port of SLES9 (for XEN). The option being considered here is to support fully virtualized SLES9 with PV drivers for enhanced performance. The two options described above are the two extremes in the spectrum of what can be done. Enlightening is closer to full virtualization with some key functionality exposed as hypervisor APIS. I would like to prototype "Enlightened SLES9" on Xen and evaluate the performance of Enlightened SLES9.
