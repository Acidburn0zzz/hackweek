    • Evolution Address Book Indicator Plugin

    Tags: Idea, Evolution

    Description

    An Evolution Plugin to assist users to quickly determine whether an email address is saved in their address book. When looking at your mailbox folder, the "From" column could be color-coded according to user preferences.

    For example, an incoming email message comes from someone not in your address book would be left at standard black. But if the message comes from someone in your Personal Address Book, it would be color-coded blue, and if it is from someone in your Business Address Book, it would color-coded green, and so on.

    Advantage: Quickly scan your inbox folder and make sure all important addresses are saved to your address book.
