    • Evolution RSS Signature Plugin

    Tags: Idea, Evolution

    Description

    Either extend the Signature Plugin or create a new Plugin. The idea of the RSS Plugin is to boost social networking or business advertising through the RSS Signature Plugin. For example, if you host a blog site, your signature could display the title of your latest post.

    An Example Signature

    Sincerely, * Joe Smith *Visit my Blog at www.someblog.com *Latest Blog Article __My Latest Blog Article Title__ (<<

    Since some of us have multiple mail accounts as well as host multiple sites (I do), such a plugin should be configurable on a per mail account or per signature basis.

    People

    Bryen Yunashko originated this idea. No one is currently working on implementing this idea (it's up for grabs!).
