    • Extend KTeaTime for pizza and other contents of the snack-box

    Tags: InProgress, Desktop, openSUSE, KDE

    Description

    KTeaTime currently shows a cup of coffee. It could be expanded to display pizzas and other hacker nutrients. Having an ability to prevent multiple trips to the kitchen to re-make tea, fire brigade visits to Maxtorhof and un-checked-on babies might improve openSUSE hacker productivity.

    People

    Will Stephenson originated this idea. It's mostly implemented but never got onto the distro.
