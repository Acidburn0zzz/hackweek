    • Framework for adding multimedia codecs

    Tags: Idea, web-service, codecs, multimedia, mp3, video

    Description

    Multimedia codecs are often not includeable in linux distributions, due to various licensing restrictions. Most of the licensing restrictions hinge on redistribution of codecs. If we can remove ourselves as "redistributor" and enable the end-user to legally download codecs directly from an authorised distributor, we will have finally created a mechanism to legally and easily enable multimedia playback on openSUSE.

    From the legal/business side, we are already working with (re)distributors of codecs. Technically, we need a way to make it easy to click/install a codec or codec bundle in such a way as that the functions the codec enables are made available immediately to all multimedia applications.

    People

    Ciaran Farrell is working on this but not from a technical aspect. Juergen Weigert has some good ideas.
