    • 'Live' transparent laptop screen

    Tags: Idea, PimpMyDesktop, Desktop

    Description

    A while ago there was a craze for setting your desktop wallpaper to a view of what was behind the computer, then taking a picture of this and blogging it.

    Combine this with a laptop's lid-mounted webcam and a compositing window manager and you have the feature to sell thousands of SLEDs to easily impressed CIOs, I know it.

    People

    Will Stephenson originated this idea. No one is currently working on implementing this idea (it's up for grabs!).
