    Description

    po-mode

    po-mode helps editing .po files; it is part of the gettext package.

    po-mode is out-dated, it does not support ''msgctxt'' fields that were introduced with gettext 0.16. [Update: Bruno pointed out that po-mode already honors msgctxt.]

    Editing plural entries does not work reliable--it often clashes with fuzzy markers.

    [Reminder by Bruno:] gettext/Admin/plans:

    po-mode.el: Editing of msgstr[i] plural forms, and setting of formula.

    D.h. es muss dem Übersetzer möglich sein, jedes der msgstr[i] einzeln zu editieren, und deren Anzahl sollte eine Funktion der Plural-Formel sein. Wenn keine da ist, sollte man sich die passende mittels 'msginit' holen.

    Better translation memory support is desirable. Finally, add some eye-candy (mini-icons).
