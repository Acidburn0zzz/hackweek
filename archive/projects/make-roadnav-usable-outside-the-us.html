    • Make Roadnav usable outside the US

    Tags: Idea, GPS

    Description

    [[http://roadnav.sourceforge.net/|Roadnav]] is an open-source GPS navigation system using street vector data provided by the US government to guide you to your destination. It does support several other data sources, e.g. [[http://www.openstreetmap.org/|OpenStreetMap]] (needs a patch to get the place names right, ask me if you need it), but it is rather US-centric in its internal data representation, and it has problems mapping the information provided by OSM to it. OSM is not perfect either as most roads available there do not have a connection to the city they are in, so good luck if you are looking for a "Hauptstrasse". Maybe it is necessary to aquire a new source of data, e.g. by vectorizing publically available maps.

    People

    Ulrich Hecht originated this idea. No one is currently working on implementing this idea (it's up for grabs!).
