    • Offer Full System Encryption (Root / Home / Swap) with the Installer

    Tags: Idea, Security, Encryption, openSUSE

    Description

    openSUSE should have an option for full system encryption when installing from DVD. Currently, the installer offers only encryption of individual partitions. There's no workflow to do a full encryption (however, a how-to to do it after installing openSUSE exists, but it's rather complicated and involves moving partitions and their content around).

    (Fedora 9 offers this. It's even turned ON by default.)
