    • openSUSE Paste - openSUSE Connect connection

    Description

    Currently openSUSE Paste works totally independently to the rest of openSUSE infrastructure. It would be nice if it could authenticate itself against openSUSE Connect. That way registered users could have list of their pastes, delete them or create new ones (skip spam filtering and use users login).
