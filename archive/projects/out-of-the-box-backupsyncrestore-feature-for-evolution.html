    • Out of the box backup/sync/restore solution for Evolution

    Tags: InProgress, Desktop, SLED, Evolution

    Description

    Evolution 2.11.x has a backup/restore plugin that tar/untar the whole .evolution directory. Instead, the idea is to make a out-of-the-box solution that would take backups, sync/merge backups and restore them on fresh installs.

    People

    Veerapuram Varadhan originated this idea and would be working on it as part of Hack Week!
