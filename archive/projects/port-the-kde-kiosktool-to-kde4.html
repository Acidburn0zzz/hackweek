    • Port the KDE kiosktool to KDE4

    Tags: Idea, openSUSE, SLES, SLED, SLERT, SLEPOS, SLETC, kde

    Description

    The Kiosktool is a very important tool for larger deployments of KDE. Currently Kiosktool is not ported to KDE4, which will become a large handicap for KDE when choosing a desktop environment in these settings.

    I work at Cendio AB in Sweden (http://www.cendio.com) with thin client systems. We deliver Linux desktops, mostly for school use, and I can't stress enough how important kiosktool has been in these installations.

    People

    Inge Wallin originated this idea. No one is currently working on implementing this idea (it's up for grabs!).
