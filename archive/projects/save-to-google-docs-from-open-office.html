    • Save to Google Docs from Open Office

    Tags: Idea, web20, office

    Description

    Add a "Save or Export to Google Docs" feature that uploads a document to your google documents account. Another feature might be "Import from Google Docs", which would query your google account, and get a list of the available files for import.

    I often save my documents on my google docs account, because it allows me to easily share between computers, and to easily publish. It also allows me to edit the documents if I am on a machine without OOo.

    People

    Jackson Harper originated this idea. No one is currently working on implementing this idea (it's up for grabs!).
