    • Pre-installed Screen capture software - not just an image but video with audio based on open codec

    Tags: Idea

    Description

    While taking screen captures, i felt the need for a pre-installed video capture software Got the need/idea when there was a need for it. To know the need, check out [[a-cool-openglopenal-based-3d-game-that-has-suselinux-written-all-over-it]]

    In fact there are some cool tools available to do them, but I guess most of them requires downloading codecs with licensing issues, my idea is to create a software which can do it out of the box, say it could use dirac or theora and bundle it with the distribution
