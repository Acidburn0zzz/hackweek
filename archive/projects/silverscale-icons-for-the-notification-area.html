    • Silverscale icons for the notification area

    Tags: InProgress, PimpMyDesktop, openSUSE, SLED, icon, theme, spec, HW-Designer

    Description

    Current notification areas already get pretty crowded. With some applets nearby the situation gets worse. The nice colorfullness of our beautiful icons create a situation where it gets harder and harder to grasp important notifications.

    That's why we should create silverscale replacements for notification area icons and some applet icons.

    * Silverscale, because we are using the 6 Tango silver colors, they should be enough. * Silverscale, because could look very classy and professional. * Exception to the rule: Warnings etc **should** still use a bit of red or orange * Make it and addon , so people can use regular colorful Tango if they want to and to fall back to colorful icons if no silverscale icons exist.

    Goals: * Make the panel lock more professional when many icons are present * Make warning icons more visible because those are the only icons using colors other than silver. * Fit into Tango Icon Theme

    List of applets that could need Silverscale icons:

    * network-manager * battery applet * weather applet (this will be hard) * sound volume applet * im applets * deskbar applet * brightness applet * resolution switcher

    People

    Joerg Kress originated this idea. Joerg Kress is working on this idea. Help from Designers appreciated.
