    • process-to-disk suspend

    Tags: Idea, Mobile, LowPower, UnixFeature, Kernel

    Description

    On usual suspend to disk all other userspace processes are frozen and the whole (used/allocated) memory is put to disk. What i'd like to have is something similar, but with just of a finer granularity, being able to suspend-to-disk single processes or groups of processes.

    The usecases i have in mind reach from making boot faster (just resuming the services processes instead of starting them from scratch - wherer possible/appropriate), over easy restoring of previous desktop sessions of apps (if they e.g. have no possibility to fully save their current state) .. to process migration between (also perhaps virtualized) machines.

    For the desktop / on notebooks we already have that possibility wiht usual suspend-to-disk (coming back exactly where i left), but often i'd like to have that feature just for some specific applications.

    I'm not fully sure if something similar perhaps already exists. Please tell me if you know more about this.
