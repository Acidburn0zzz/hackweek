    • Support openSUSE-Education

    Tags: opensuse, community, Education, inprogress, InBuildService, TryMe, yast

    Tags: HelpWanted, HW-Hacker, HW-Tester, HW-Docs, HW-Packaging

    Description

    [[http://en.opensuse.org/Education|openSUSE-Education]] wants to support openSUSE in schools and educational projects.

    The project is alive since 2006 and needs support from outside (teachers, packagers, enthusiasts) and inside Novell.

    The project is a main project, collecting particular projects like:

    * [[sugar-up-opensuse]] * [[http://en.opensuse.org/LTSP|LTSP]] * [[http://en.opensuse.org/Education/Development#Packaging|Packaging]] * [[http://en.opensuse.org/Education/Development/Edu-CD|Education-DVD]] * [[http://www.openschoolserver.net|OpenSchoolServer]] * [[http://en.opensuse.org/Education/Development#YaST_Modules|YaST Modules]] * ...

    The project has already released Add On DVDs for openSUSE 10.2 and openSUSE 10.3 - and is currently working on the Add On DVD for openSUSE 11.0 (see [[http://en.opensuse.org/Education/Edu-CD/Roadmap|Roadmap]] for details).

    Success stories: * [[http://linkat.xtec.cat/portal/|Linkat]] A project from the catalan education minister, uses packages from the openSUSE Education project on their SLED 10 based product. * Schools in germany get thousands of openSUSE 10.2 boxes and the openSUSE-Education DVD [[http://www.openschoolserver.net/neuigkeiten-mainmenu-2/1-latest/76-kostenlose-opensuse-102-fchulen|for free]] * many schools all over the world are already using openSUSE in their daily business

    People

    Founder of openSUSE-Education was [[http://en.opensuse.org/User:Jasujt|James Tremblay]]. The project is driven by the [[http://en.opensuse.org/Education/Team|Education-Team]]. Currently [[http://en.opensuse.org/User:lrupp|Lars Vogdt]] is the technical contact person.

    * Have a look at the [[http://en.opensuse.org/Education|Wiki-Pages]] to get a first overview. * The project has it's own [[http://www.opensuse-education.org/|Website]] containing News and informations for developers ([[http://bugs.opensuse-education.org/|Bugzilla]],[[https://svn.linux-schulserver.de/viewvc/?root=openSUSE-Education|SVN]], ...). * Subprojects like [[http://en.opensuse.org/Easy-LTSP|Easy-LTSP]] might have [[http://developer.novell.com/wiki/index.php/Easy-LTSP|additional ressources]] - just ask on the [[http://lists.opensuse.org/opensuse-edu/|mailinglist]] for details.
