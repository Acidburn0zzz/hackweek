    Description

    openSUSE-Education wants to support openSUSE in schools and educational projects.

    The project is alive since 2006 and needs support from outside (teachers, packagers, enthusiasts) and inside Novell.

    The project is a main project, collecting particular projects like:

    • sugar-up-opensuse
    • LTSP
    • Packaging
    • Education-DVD
    • OpenSchoolServer
    • YaST Modules
    • ...

    The project has already released Add On DVDs for openSUSE 10.2 and openSUSE 10.3 - and is currently working on the Add On DVD for openSUSE 11.0 (see Roadmap for details).

    Success stories:

    • Linkat A project from the catalan education minister, uses packages from the openSUSE Education project on their SLED 10 based product.
    • Schools in germany get thousands of openSUSE 10.2 boxes and the openSUSE-Education DVD for free
    • many schools all over the world are already using openSUSE in their daily business
