    • task tracker

     

    Description

    Periodically check with user to see what they're doing, and make suggestions of things to work on. Compile end of day and end of week work reports. Requests a list at end of day for tomorrow's priorities. Keeps track of time on each task. Option to add things to do in the future (when's a good time today/this week/this month/this year?), which is where suggestions come from. Keeps track of schedule (so you don't get notifications while not at work). Integrate with calendar maybe, to keep track of meetings?

    I'd like to integrate it into gnome3 notifications, sort of like pidgin. I'll post sources on github.
