    • tasky

    Tags: Desktop, Web20, Mono, InProgress, InBuildService, TryMe

    Description

    Tasky is a simple task management app for the Linux Desktop. The idea is most TODO lists and applications are too complicated. Tasky is simple and integrated into the desktop. It also will sync and stay in sync with Remember The Milk.

    People

    Calvin Gaisford, Boyd Timothy, Brian Merrell and Andrew Jorgensen are working on this project
