    • Time sensitive desktop lighting

    Tags: Idea, Desktop, SLED, openSUSE, HelpWanted, Personality, PimpMyDesktop

    Description

    Similarly to the iGoogle themes, It would be great if our desktop themes and wallpaper for example could be influenced by the current time of day.

    This light could be taking into account local sunrise and sunset times based on location information (coordinates from the new clock applet for example)

    People

    Guy Lunardi originated this idea. In order for this idea to go anywhere, someone will need to take the lead on it please. It's up for grab! Much help requested for this idea.
