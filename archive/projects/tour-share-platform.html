    • Tour Share Platform

    Tags: Idea, Community, web20, Drago

    Description

    Lots of people like to go by bike, hike or walk, go by car or even boat. All these people have their secret (or not so) favorite trails.

    Quite similar to video share platforms we could set up a tour-share platform. Interested people add a short description of their trip and describe the way they went, drove or whatever. In addition to that, photos or videos give more ideas of the landscape and details. The author can pick 'well known places' along the tour and name them.

    Have not investigated yet, but I can imagine that we could use a free format to specify the tour data and along with affordable GPS stuff it should be possible to record that data during the trip?!

    The peace that is missing IMO in that idea is a real reason why people should really enter their trip data - that probably can be a lot of work, more than just uploading my latest my-dog-in-the-swimming-pool video. We have to find a way to make the membership of the community something people like to have :-)

    Couldn't that be a cool web20 thing?
