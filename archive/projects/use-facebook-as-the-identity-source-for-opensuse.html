    • Use Facebook as the identity source for OpenSUSE

    Tags: Idea

    Description

    In my opinion, creating a new user identity to become a registered user on OpenSUSE is a barrier. Besides the fact I'm tired of maintaining what seems like a bazillion internet identities. Facebook is fast becoming the largest social network site on the Internet and best of all they support an open application development platform. It would be relatively easy for OpenSUSE to leverage identities from Facebook rather than maintaining it's own identity store. With over 40 million current users and growing at 3% a month many new potential OpenSUSE community members will already have a Facebook identity which will aid in quickly provisioning the user.

    People

    Brady Anderson originated this idea. No one is currently working on implementing this idea (it's up for grabs!).
