    • utrace

    Tags: InProgress, Kernel, DevTool, reversing, judgeunixattack

    Description

    Roland McGrath maintains a patch that a) reimplements the current ptrace mechanism, b) allows for cool things like attaching more tracers to a process, having different tracing engines available (eg. the ptrace() reimplementation is just an utrace engine), etc. The idea is to have a look at utrace, build it and try some of the cool things :)

    People

    Michal Marek originated this idea and will have a look at it during the hackweek (but help is welcome!)
