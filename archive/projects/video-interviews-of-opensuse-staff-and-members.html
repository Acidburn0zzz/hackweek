    • Video Interviews of openSUSE Staff and Members

    Tags: Idea, Community, openSUSE, SLES, SLED, SLERT, SLEPOS, SLETC, HelpWanted, InProgress, TryMe

    Description

    The aim is to get members of openSUSE staff in Nurenberg on film so people in the community know who does what and put faces to names/nicks. SUSE have never self advertised well, so maybe the community can help :-) The aim is to do this until all corners of the openSUSE globe are covered.

    People

    Andrew Wafaa, originator and initial cameraman/director/producer/editor
