    • Video sharing Desktop or other material

    Tags: Idea, Desktop, Network, Mobile, Multimedia, filesharing

    Description

    Not everyone, especially desktop user, want to setup a server like application to share with others, even Samba share may seem difficult. In order to make this possible what we could do is able let user to boardcast any material and acting as some kind of service or external material over other user's dbus. Video will multicast over the network after timeout or after everybody in the group reply to this boardcast service.

    People

    Alex Lau ( AvengerMoJo ) originated this idea. No one is currently working on implementing this idea (it's up for grabs!).
