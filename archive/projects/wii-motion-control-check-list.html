    • Wii Motion Control Check List

    Tags: Idea, Desktop, Community, Web20, Mobile, games

    Description

    With Wii Control, we get two 3-D controller and one joystick and one cross button, A+B+C+Z button, plus, minus and home button. With all that we can do a motion combo on all action and application or command. With that we can also make a community checklist sharing, show who's check list is the best usage. And then we can have a global competition on the best 10 choose of use combo hits the fastest. They can play with work, workout while working.
