    • Workflow F-Spot/Flickr Upload

    Tags: Idea, Desktop, Photo, F-Spot

    Description

    I would love to see a small utility that plugged-in to F-Spot that allows me to take care of my entire "go-to-web" experience.

    My typical workflow:

    - Plug my camera in to my computer with USB - Import them into my photo application - Single out the good photos and delete the blurry ones - Add titles/tags/comments to the photos (but not always) - Convert them from RAW format to JPEG - Upload them to my Flickr account

    I'd love to see something to take care of this all in one shot.

    People

    Boyd Timothy originated this idea. No one is currently working on implementing this idea (it's up for grabs!). Someone give this idea some love and make it work! :D
