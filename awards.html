    Awards for the best projects of Hack Week 9

    The Hack Week awards recognize the greatest, most promising, or craziest projects and ideas of Hack Week. At Hack Week 9 we will give awards in the following categories:

    • Best overall project
    • Best team effort
    • Best upstream contribution
    • Best openSUSE project
    • Best innovation
    • Most glorious failure
    • Best non-software project
    • Best picture
    • Best video

    The jury deciding about the awards is formed by the SUSE distinguished engineers, Olaf Kirch, Michael Meeks, and Lars Marowsky-Brée.

    We will have prizes for the winners of the awards. Two winners will get a Chromebook, others will get vouchers for online shopping.

    Finally, the most promising projects will get additional hacking time to continue on their projects, and make them ready for prime time as part of a SUSE or openSUSE product.

    To be eligible for the awards please make sure that your project is listed on the Hack Week Wiki, and it contains all the information and pointers to code and other material, the jury will need to judge it.
