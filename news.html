    What is happening at Hack Week 9?

    24th Apr 2013 Get your Hack Week projects ready for the awards

    Hack Week 9 is over and we saw a lot of exciting projects. Now the only bit missing is choosing the winners of the Hack Week awards.

    Please make sure that your project pages in the Hack Week Wiki are up to date and contain all the information the Hack Week award jury needs by end of this week. We'll use this as the base for choosing the winners.

     hackweek.suse.com
    9th Apr 2013 Record your Hack Week

    We are on day 2 of Hack Week 9. Lots of interesting projects are going on. Let's make sure that they are not happening in the hidden, but that they are visible to our colleagues and everybody else who is interested.

    Make sure your project is on the Hack Week 9 Wiki.

    It doesn't matter if it's a big project of a team of developers porting the distribution to a new platform, or if it's just the one small bug in your desktop you always wanted to fix. Just make sure that there is a trail of what you are doing on the Wiki.

    Another great way to document Hack Week is pictures and movies. There even are prizes for the best ones as part of the Hack Week awards.

    Record people in action, take photos of what you built, or portrait people hacking away on their projects. We have a Hack Week Flickr group.

    Finally blog, tweet, dent, whatever, to let your colleagues, friends, and the community know what is happening. We use the hashtag #hackweek9.

     hackweek.suse.com
